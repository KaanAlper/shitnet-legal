SHITNET — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This page describes how to delete your account and data in the SHITNET app (developer: Kaan Alper Karaaslan).

How to Delete

Send us an email, and within 30 days all your data is permanently deleted:

📧 Send Deletion Request

Or manually:

  1. Write to: kaanalperkaraaslan@gmail.com
  2. Subject: SHITNET Account Deletion Request
  3. Body must include:
    • Your username (e.g., BoktanLord347)
    • Email associated with your account
    • Brief sentence confirming deletion request

Process

  • You'll receive a confirmation email within 3 business days
  • Your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • A completion notice is sent when deletion is complete
